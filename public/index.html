How it works?
  • 1

    Create Nodes Using $HONE

    Using our native token HONE users will be able to create Nodes from four tiers and earn between 1-1.75% daily.

  • 2

    Compound, Claim, or Merge Nodes

    On our platform, users will be able to claim their rewards to sell for a profit, compound their rewards into additional nodes, or merge/upgrade existing tiers nodes into higher tiers. 

  • 3

    Rent Your Nodes

    On our platform we'll have a unique feature, where users can "rent" their nodes, essentially receiving a fixed monthly payment in stables from other users( who are unable to purchase a full node), in exchange for the returns from their nodes.

What is Harmony Nodes and what makes this project unique?

 Harmony Nodes is the first DAAS on the harmony ecosystem. Using our native token $HONE, users will be able to create nodes from several tiers and earn passive income at 1-1.75% daily. One of the things that sets us apart is that users will be able to combine lower tiers of nodes into higher tiers. So if a user starts with one pico node and works up to two of them, he can combine them into 1 nano node and receive a higher daily return. Since the presale is only pico nodes, if users buy more than one they'll be able to merge them into the higher tiers at launch.

What do whitelisted members get?

Whitelisted members will be able to participate to the presale, where they'll be able to purchase nfts redeemable for nodes at a fixed price. Those who own an nft will be able to claim nodes directly from the front end at launch at a 1:1 rate (one node per nft owned).

How will the presale work?

In the presale, members will be able to mint nfts redeemable for nodes at a fixed price. Those who own an nft will be able to claim nodes directly from the front end at launch at a 1:1 rate (one node per nft owned).As mentioned above, the presale is only for pico nodes, so if users buy more than one they'll be able to merge them into the higher tiers nodes at launch.The price per nft will be $200. Whitelisted members will be able to mint up to 10.
